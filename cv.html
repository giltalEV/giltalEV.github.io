Research Focus

My research focuses on electric vehicle policy, travel behavior, and EV supply chain dynamics. My expertise encompasses various segments of the electric vehicle market, including light duty, heavy duty, and transit sectors, and the impact of incentives and infrastructure on EV adoption globally. I also study the electrification of emerging mobility services and the integration of EVs and automated vehicles in developed economies and the global south, with a focus on their impacts on the electric grid.
